SILENT CLAUSE RULE: If a clause is NOT in the lease → write "Lease is silent." (never "Not found").

STYLE: Section reference first, then cite, then content.
Pattern: "Sec. X.X: [content]. [CITE: page N, section X.X]"

CURRENCY: $1,234.56 (two decimals for non-round numbers).

FIELD EXTRACTION RULES (follow exactly):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[TENANT_LEGAL_NAME]
Tenant Legal Name is found in the Lease Summary on Page 1 under 'Tenant' or in the preamble 'by and between' clause. Extract the LEGAL entity name (e.g., 'Dan Hong Lu'), NOT the trade name/DBA. Also check Sec. 1.3 for Tenant definition.

[LANDLORD_LEGAL_NAME]
Landlord Legal Name is found in the Lease Summary Pg. 1 under 'Landlord' or in the preamble. Extract FULL legal entity name including entity type (e.g., 'Bella-AZ, LLLP'). Also check Sec. 1.2 for Landlord definition.

[DBA]
DBA (Doing Business As) is found in Sec. 1.4 under 'Trade Name' or in the Lease Summary under 'DBA' or 'Trade Name'. This is the business operating name, NOT the legal entity. Example: 'Pier 99 Boiling Seafood & Bar'.

[PROPERTY_ADDRESS]
Property Address is found in Lease Summary Pg. 1 or Sec. 1.1. Extract FULL address including street, city, state, and zip (e.g., '4040 E Bell Rd, Phoenix, AZ 85032').

[PREMISES_SIZE]
Combined Square Footage / Premises Size is in Lease Summary Pg. 1 or Sec. 1.5. Format as number with commas and two decimals (e.g., '4,441.00'). Check for re-measurement clauses in Sec. 1.5.

[LEASE_TYPE]
Lease Type is determined from NNN/CAM provisions. If TT pays base rent + real estate taxes + insurance + maintenance (NNN), type is 'Net Lease'. Check Sec. 1.9 for TT's payment of maintenance expenses. Also look for 'NNN' references anywhere in the lease.

[TYPE_OF_ORGANIZATION]
Type of Organization is found in the preamble or Lease Summary. Examples: 'An Individual', 'a Delaware LLC', 'a California corporation', 'a limited partnership'. Check the entity description after the tenant or landlord name.

[STATE_OF_ORGANIZATION]
State of Organization is found in the preamble entity description (e.g., 'a Delaware LLC' → State: Delaware). If individual, use the state from TT's address. If not stated, check Guarantor address state.

[GUARANTOR]
Guarantor field is a Yes/No boolean. Check Lease Summary for 'Lease Guarantee' section and look for Exhibit C (Guaranty Agreement). If ANY guarantor exists → 'Yes'. Also check Sec. 1.8(e).

[GUARANTOR_NAME]
Guarantor Name is in Lease Summary under 'Lease Guarantee' or Sec. 1.8(e) or Exhibit C. Extract the person/entity name only. Often the same as tenant name for individuals.

[LEASE_DATES]
LEASE DATES — CRITICAL: Sign Date is the latest execution date (check signature pages). Lease Start/LCD may be conditional on delivery of possession — check Sec. 1.7(a) and Effective Date clause. RCD is often different from LCD — check Sec. 1.8(b) for RCD formula (e.g., '180 days following delivery' or 'date TT opens for business'). LED is calculated from RCD — e.g., '15 years after the 1st day of the 1st full month following RCD'. Always note when dates are assumed/conditional in Queries & Assumptions.

[HOLDOVER]
Holdover % is in Sec. 4.2 or equivalent holdover section. Extract the percentage (e.g., '125%'). Also note the holdover tenancy type (e.g., 'tenancy at sufferance'). The holdover clause describes what happens if TT remains after lease expiration.

[LATE_FEE]
Late Fee details are in Sec. 28 or equivalent. Extract: (1) Calculation type (% or flat), (2) Percentage/Amount (e.g., '5%'), (3) Grace period in days (e.g., '10'), (4) Due day (e.g., '1'). Note any postmark presumptions. Format: Type='% Owed-Total', Percent='5%', Grace='10'.

[SECURITY_DEPOSIT]
Security Deposit amount is in Lease Summary under 'Security Deposit' and Sec. 1.8(d). Extract exact dollar amount. Also check Sec. 27 for: interest bearing (Yes/No), offset rights, restore obligation, return conditions. Format amount as '$28,126.00'.

[RENT_SCHEDULE]
RENT SCHEDULE — CRITICAL: Extract EVERY year/period of the rent schedule. Check Lease Summary 'Yearly Rent' section and Sec. 1.8(a-b). For annual escalations (e.g., 3% increase), calculate each year separately. Include: Unit, From Date, To Date, Monthly Amount, Annual Amount, Annual Per SF. The first period starts at RCD. If RCD falls mid-month, first payment is prorated per 30-day month. MUST also extract rent for Option periods if Sec. 1.8(c) states rent increases through 'Term and any Option Extensions'.

[RENT_ESCALATION]
Rent Escalation / Increase is in Sec. 1.8(c). Extract the escalation formula (e.g., '3% every year throughout the Term and any Option Extensions'). This applies to base rent increases year-over-year.

[INSURANCE]
Insurance requirements are in Sec. 13.1-13.4. Extract EACH type separately: (1) Commercial General Liability with limits (e.g., '$3,000,000 per occurrence, $5,000,000 total'), (2) Workers Comp (Yes/No), (3) Property Insurance, (4) Use/Occupancy Insurance, (5) Dram Shop if liquor. Also extract: deductible cap, additional insured parties, cancellation notice days (e.g., '30'), rating requirement, waiver of subrogation (Sec. 13.4).

[CONTACTS]
CONTACTS — CRITICAL: Extract ALL contact details for: (1) Tenant Notice — from Lease Summary 'Tenant' and Sec. 1.3, 1.11, 25. Include: Name, Address 1, Address 2, City, State, Zip, Country, Phone, Email. (2) Landlord Notice — from Lease Summary 'Landlord' and Sec. 1.2, 1.10, 25. (3) Payee — often same as Landlord. (4) Guarantor contact — from Exhibit C or same as Tenant if individual. ALWAYS extract full addresses with suite numbers, all phone numbers, and email addresses.

[RENEWAL_OPTIONS]
OPTIONS — CRITICAL: Check Lease Summary 'Option Term(s)' and Sec. 1.7(b), 4.1. For EACH option extract: (1) Option Type (Renewal), (2) Option Status (Active), (3) Renewal Term dates, (4) Notice period (e.g., '12 months' = 365 days Latest Notice), (5) Who Initiates (Tenant/Landlord), (6) Renewal Rent formula from Sec. 1.8(c). NOTE: If Lease Summary and Body of Lease contradict on number of options, use Body of Lease (BLP) and note in Queries. Calculate option period rent using the same escalation formula.

[CAM_OPEX]
CAM/OpEx/NNN details are in Lease Summary 'Tenant's Payment of Maintenance Expenses' and Sec. 1.9. For NNN leases, TT is responsible for: parking lot, landscaping, roof repairs (structural and non-structural), HVAC repairs and replacement, real estate taxes, and insurance. Note whether TT pays 100% directly or a pro-rata share. Also note if there are any CAM caps.

[ASSIGNMENT_SUBLETTING]
Assignment & Subletting clause is in Sec. 7.1-7.4. Extract: (1) With Consent transfers — LL consent required, not unreasonably withheld. (2) Without Consent transfers — list ALL permitted transfers (mergers, parent entities, franchisees, etc.). (3) Continuing liability — does TT remain liable after transfer? (4) Assumption requirements from Sec. 7.4. Use standard abbreviations: TT, LL, w/, w/out.

[DEFAULT_CLAUSE]
Default clause is in Sec. 22. Extract SEPARATELY: (1) Monetary Default — cure period in days after written notice (e.g., '3 days'). (2) Non-Monetary Default — cure period (e.g., '30 days'), with extension if cure cannot be completed within period. Note force majeure or threatened injury exceptions. Cite as: L, Pg. [N], Sec. 22.

[PERMITTED_USE]
Permitted Use is in Lease Summary 'Use of Premises' and Sec. 1.4, 1.6, 3.1. Extract the specific permitted use (e.g., 'Seafood Bar and Grill and for no other use w/out LL's prior written consent'). Also extract Trade Name from Sec. 1.4.

[ESTOPPEL]
Estoppel Certificate is in Sec. 17. Extract: notice period (e.g., '10 days'), who can request (LL), what must be certified (unmodified, in full force, dates rent paid), and third-party reliance rights.

[GO_DARK]
Continuous Use / Go Dark clause is in Sec. 9. Extract whether TT is prohibited from vacating/abandoning during the Term, and what constitutes a default if TT does vacate. If lease is silent, state 'Lease is silent.'

[GUARANTY_CLAUSE]
Guaranty Clause details are in Lease Summary 'Lease Guarantee', Sec. 1.8(e), and Exhibit C. Extract: Guarantor Name, Obligations scope (all monetary and non-monetary), whether unconditional and irrevocable. Cite Exhibit C for full details.

[ALTERATIONS]
Alterations clause is in Sec. 8. Extract: (1) With Consent — what requires LL consent (structural alterations), (2) Without Consent — dollar threshold for non-structural interior alterations (e.g., '$10,000'), (3) Notice period before commencement (e.g., '7 days'), (4) Removal obligations at termination.

[SUBORDINATION]
Subordination/SNDA is in Sec. 15. Extract: whether lease is subordinate to mortgages/deeds of trust, non-disturbance protection (lease survives foreclosure so long as TT not in default), and attornment to transferee obligations.

[UTILITIES]
Utilities clause is in Sec. 10. Extract what TT pays: gas, electricity, water, sewage, air conditioning, telephone. Note if payment is prior to delinquency and when responsibility starts (after possession transferred).

[PERCENTAGE_RENT]
Percentage Rent / Gross Sales is in Sec. 41. Extract: reporting frequency (monthly), report due date (30 days after CY end), definition of Gross Sales (all sales, mail/online orders, deposits not refunded, sublessee sales). Note whether audited report is required.

[TT_REPAIR]
TT's Repair obligations are in Sec. 11.2. Extract all maintenance items TT is responsible for: keeping premises clean, health regulation compliance, maintaining/repairing premises including HVAC and structural portions. Cite specific subsections.

[ROOF_REPAIR]
Roof Repair is in Sec. 1.9 and 8.1. Check if roof penetrations require LL consent (Sec. 8.1). Check Sec. 1.9 for whether TT is responsible for roof repairs (both structural and non-structural). This is especially important for NNN leases.

[PROHIBITED_USE]
Prohibited Use is in Sec. 3.2. Extract specific prohibitions such as Hazardous Materials storage/use/transport. Cite the section and summarize key restrictions.

[BROKERS]
Broker information is in Sec. 40. If no brokers were engaged, state 'N/A' for both Landlord Broker and Tenant Agent. Extract any hold harmless provisions. Cite as L, Pg. [N], Sec. 40.

[TI_ALLOWANCE]
TI Allowance is in Sec. 2 and 11.1. If TT accepts premises 'AS IS', note this explicitly. If there is a specific dollar allowance, extract the amount and payment deadline.

[QUERIES_ASSUMPTIONS]
QUERIES & ASSUMPTIONS — CRITICAL: Always generate a numbered list of items requiring human confirmation. Common queries: (1) Conditional dates (LCD, RCD, LED calculations), (2) Contact/billing address assumptions, (3) Guarantor address assumptions, (4) Rent period calculations (year definition, proration), (5) Option contradictions between Summary and BLP, (6) Unit creation assumptions. Format each as: '[N]. [Topic]: [Explanation]. [Assumption made] as per Client's confirmation.'

[MISCELLANEOUS]
Miscellaneous clause should capture important provisions not covered elsewhere: (1) Recapture contingencies (e.g., Sec. 44), (2) Delivery of possession timelines and termination rights (Sec. 6), (3) Re-measurement clauses (Sec. 1.5), (4) Any unique provisions. Also summarize Option details here with full rent schedules for each option period.

[DOCUMENT_LIST]
Document List must include EVERY document with: Document Name (e.g., 'Building Lease'), Document Date (MM/DD/YYYY), and Notes (e.g., 'By and between [LL entity] (LL) and [TT entity], (TT).'). Include original lease, all amendments, exhibits, guarantees.

[LEASE_TERM]
Lease term is found in the early sections of the lease (e.g., Article I, Section 1.01 or 1.02), described as beginning from the 'LCD' (Lease Commencement Date) and continuing until the 'LED' (Lease Expiration Date). Look for language defining the full period of tenancy.

[RENTAL_TERM]
Rental term (the period during which rent is actually paid) is found in the same section as the lease term but is specifically defined as [N] full consecutive calendar months following the RCD, not the LCD. Extract this separately from the overall lease term.

[EXPIRATION_DATE]
Expiration date (ED) is found in Article I, Section 1.01(K) or equivalent, defined as a specific number of full months from the RCD. Record as 'ED: [N] Full Months from the RCD.'

[DELIVERY_OF_POSSESSION]
Delivery of possession terms are found in Article I, Section 1.01(J) or equivalent. Record the trigger event for delivery (e.g., following mutual execution of the Lease).

[LEASE_YEAR_DEFINITION]
If the LCD falls on any date other than January 1, the first Lease Year runs from the RCD through December 31 of that year. Subsequent Lease Years are full calendar years. Capture this exception explicitly in the abstract.

[RENT_ABATEMENT]
Rent abatement (free rent) period is identified from the rent schedule or early lease sections. Record the specific months during which rent is abated (e.g., 'Months 1-3').

[PRORATION]
Proration is calculated at 1/30th of monthly rent per day for the partial month from the RCD through the last day of that month. After the partial month, rent is due on the first of each calendar month. Capture this formula explicitly.

[PREPAID_RENT_CAM]
Prepaid Rent and CAM amounts are found in Article I, Section 1.01(R) or equivalent. Record the total prepaid amount, the payment deadline (e.g., within [N] days of execution), and what it is applied toward (first installment of Fixed Minimum Rent and CAM).

[EXCESS_RENT_SPLIT]
Excess rent (profit on assignment/subletting above [TENANT]'s rent obligation) split is found in the assignment section. Record the percentage retained by [LANDLORD] (e.g., '[RATE]%').

[PERMITTED_TRANSFER]
Permitted transfers (assignments not requiring [LANDLORD] consent) are found in the assignment section. Record all qualifying categories: affiliates, parents, subsidiaries, entities under common control, merger/consolidation successors, and asset/stock acquirers.

[ASSIGNMENT_NOTICE]
Notice period for assignment/renewal options is found in the assignment or options section. Record as '[N] days prior to expiration of the Term.'

[ASSIGNMENT_FEE]
Assignment review fees payable to [LANDLORD] are found in the assignment section. Record the minimum and maximum fee range, who prepares the assignment document, and that fees are payable as Additional Rent upon demand.

[RENEWAL_OPTION]
Renewal options are found in Article I, Section 1.02(K-M) or equivalent. Record the number of options, the duration of each option period, and which option number is being described (e.g., '2nd of 2, [N]-year option to renew the Lease').

[INTEREST_ON_LATE_PAYMENT]
Interest on late payments is found in Article III, Section 3.06 or equivalent. Record the annual interest rate, the grace period before interest accrues, and any statutory cap on the rate.

[MONETARY_DEFAULT]
Monetary default provisions are found in Article XIV-XV, Section 19.01 or equivalent. Record the grace period (number of days after due date) before a monetary default is triggered, and list the categories of payment covered (rent, additional rent, costs, charges, fees, taxes, insurance, CAM).

[NON_MONETARY_DEFAULT]
Non-monetary default provisions are found in the same default section as monetary default. Record the cure period, the condition for avoiding default (commencement of cure within the period plus diligent completion), and the distinction from rent non-payment.

[ESTOPPEL_CERTIFICATE]
Estoppel certificate is written as 'W/in [N] days after request therefor by LL to execute in recordable form and deliver to LL an Estoppel Certificate Statement as provided by LL, in writing, certifying certain terms and conditions as set forth in Sec. [N].' Use 'W/in' for 'Within' and 'LL' for [LANDLORD].

[FAILURE_TO_OPEN]
Failure to open provisions are found in Article II, Section 2.05 or equivalent. Record the deadline for [TENANT] to open for business (number of days following LCD), the required state (fully fixtured, stocked, and staffed), and any force majeure or permit delay exceptions.

[USE_CLAUSE]
Use clause restrictions are found in Article VI, Section 7.02 or equivalent. Record prohibited uses (pornography, drug paraphernalia, amusement devices, second-hand/liquidation sales, etc.), operating hours requirements, and sidewalk use restrictions.

[REAL_PROPERTY_TAXES_PRO_RATA_SHARE]
[TENANT]'s pro rata share (PRS) of real property taxes is found in Article III, Section 3.04(A) or equivalent. Record the numerator (gross leasable area of the Leased Premises) and denominator (gross leasable building area of the S/C, excluding separately assessed freestanding buildings).

[CAM_PRO_RATA_SHARE]
[TENANT]'s pro rata share of CAM is found in Article VII, Section 8.03(A) or equivalent. The same numerator/denominator structure as real property taxes applies. Record separately from the tax pro rata share.

[CAM_INCLUSIONS]
CAM inclusions are found in Article VII-VIII, Section 8.03(B) or equivalent. Record all cost categories included in CAM: operating, maintaining, equipping, policing, protecting, lighting, repairing, and replacing the S/C Common Facilities.

[TENANCY_TYPE]
Tenancy type (co-tenancy) is found in the lease and recorded under the label 'co:'. Record the type of tenancy arrangement (e.g., 'Tenancy').
